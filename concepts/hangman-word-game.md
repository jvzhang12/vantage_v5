Hangman is a traditional word-guessing game typically played by two or more players. One player thinks of a word, phrase, or sentence and indicates how many letters it contains, often with blanks for each letter. Other players suggest individual letters. Correct guesses fill in the blanks, while incorrect guesses incrementally draw parts of a stick-figure hangman. The game ends when the word is correctly guessed or the hangman figure is completed. Hangman is used for entertainment and educational purposes, such as practicing spelling and vocabulary.
